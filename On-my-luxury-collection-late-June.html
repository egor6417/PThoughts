On my luxury collection, late June

My collection now has two aspects- things I need and things I want. Both of them I will settle by buying luxury stuff, but the two aspects differ greatly in brands.

Things I need

Because I do not have a great income, I cannot buy practical things like cardholders and bags from top-of-the-line luxury brands like Hermes, and I'll need to settle for Louis Vuitton.

Items I desire

These items are bought about twice a year, and I save up for them, and therefore can't afford to buy overpriced products from the likes of Hermes or Cartier.
